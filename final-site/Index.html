What is a West Marches-style TTRPG? A West Marches-style tabletop role-playing game (TTRPG) is a unique gaming format that emphasizes player-driven exploration and adventure in a dynamic, open-world setting. Inspired by the West Marches campaign created by Ben Robbins, this style of gameplay typically involves a large group of players, often divided into smaller parties, exploring a vast and mysterious wilderness. Unlike traditional TTRPGs with fixed storylines and regular sessions, West Marches games are characterized by their episodic nature, allowing players to schedule sessions at their convenience and pursue their own objectives within the shared game world. This site will serve as a platform for creating and maintaining our own West Marches game.

Why I'm Passionate About This: I'm passionate about West Marches-style TTRPGs because they represent the epitome of collaborative storytelling and shared imagination. As both a player and a game master, I've experienced firsthand the magic that unfolds when a diverse group of individuals comes together to craft a rich and dynamic game world. The thrill of exploration, the camaraderie forged through adversity, and the sheer joy of discovering the unknown are what drives my enthusiasm for this style of gameplay. Additionally, I'm deeply inspired by the sense of community that flourishes within West Marches campaigns, where players become not just fellow adventurers, but lifelong friends bound by a shared love for storytelling and adventure. Through hosting this project, I hope to create a welcoming space where players can unleash their creativity, forge lasting connections, and embark on epic journeys that will leave a lasting impact on their lives.
